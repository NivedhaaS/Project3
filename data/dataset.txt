We are using the U.S. Historical Climatology Network dataset made available thanks to the Global Historical Climatology Network at the National Centers for Environmental Information with NOAA

You can download the latest dataset (ghcnd_hcn.tar.gz) at https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/pub/data/ghcn/daily/
